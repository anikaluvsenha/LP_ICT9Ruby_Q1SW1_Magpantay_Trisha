ENHYPEN


ENHYPEN is a K-pop boy group under BELIFT LAB, a subsidiary company of HYBE LABELS. The group was formed in 2020 by the global survival show "I-LAND" and consists of 7 members. (From left to right in the image above: Sunghoon, Heeseung, Jungwon, Jay, Jake, Sunoo, Ni-ki.) The group name "ENHYPEN" means that the seven members connect to grow and discover one another as well as connect with people through their music, similar to a hyphen which connects words.

TOP 5 Reasons Why I Love Enhypen

  1. Their TOP TIER discography
    • They work with a lot of genres, and the members participate in the songwriting and production process
    • They are popular for hits like "Bite Me," "FEVER," and "Drunk-Dazed" but I recommend listening to their latest album ROMANCE:UNTOLD.

  2. All of the members are equally talented
    • All members are certified all-rounders who can dance well and even sing live. Some members even excel in other things like figure skating (Sunghoon), Taekwondo (Jungwon), and guitar (Jay.)
    • Recommended performances:Fatal Trouble, NI-KI AOTM (he has a solo because he's the main dancer,) Bills.

  3. VISUALS.
    • There is no such thing as a visual hole in Enhypen. People need to appreciate them for both looks and talent.
    • Notable photoshoots:240729 Vogue Korea, 240729 Vogue Korea (2)

  4. Lore
    • Enhypen is also known for their vampire concept and this ties to their fictional webnovel series where every member has their own character.
    • Main storyline: DARK MOON: The Blood Altar, Prequel: DARK MOON: Children of Vamfield

  5. They're entertaining and fun to watch
    • The members have their own unique personality and sense of humor. I really like the dynamic the members have with one another.
    • ENHYPEN's very own variety show: EN-O'CLOCK
